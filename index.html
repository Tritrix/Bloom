About

Bloom was founded in 2014 by Sunjoy Prabakaran and Padma Prabakaran. Bloom is a training academy for Soft skills, life skills, work life balance for individuals and corporate institutions.

We conduct workshops and hold training exercises for coroprates in the IT and Banking industry.
